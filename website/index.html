Bayesian Inference for
Absolute Quantitation of Mitochondrial DNA

A method for modelling the error in absolute quantitation of mitochondrial DNA (mtDNA)
from single-cell data, based on an underlying Hidden Markov Model and inference using the
pseudo-marginal particle Metropolis Hastings algorithm.
